# Latvia 🇱🇻

## State Film Institutions
- [National Film Centre of Latvia](https://www.nkc.gov.lv/en)
- [Ministry of Culture](National Film Centre)

## Film Archives, Collections, and Databases
- [Latvian State Archive of Audiovisual Documents](https://www.arhivi.gov.lv/en/department/latvian-state-archive-audiovisual-documents)
	- [See, Hear Latvia!](https://www.redzidzirdilatviju.lv/en/) -- Digital archive of audiovisual, photo and audio documents of the Latvian State Archives of Audiovisual Documents.
- [Riga Film Museum Archive](https://www.kinomuzejs.lv/en/krajums/krajuma-apraksts/)
- [Filmas.lv -en](https://www.filmas.lv/en/latvian-film-classics/) -- Rich collection of restored films (mainly from the Soviet era) available worldwide
- [Filmas.lv -lv](https://www.filmas.lv/) -- The Latvian Section provides access to a majority of fiction, documentary, and animated films produced in Latvia. It is accessible from Latvia and partially from Latvian libraries.
- [latfilma.lv](https://latfilma.lv/) -- The content of this abandoned site hosted by Latvian Filmmakers' Union was integrated into filmas.lv and the NKC. However, you can still find some original articles by visiting the site via the Web Archive.
- [National Library of Latvia](https://lnb.gov.lv/en/)
	- [Digital periodicals collection](https://periodika.lndb.lv/) -- mainly in Latvian and Russian

## Film Festivals
- [Riga International Film Festival](https://rigaiff.lv/en/) -- successor of "Arsenals"
- [2ANNAS](https://2annas.lv/en) -- Riga International Short Film Festival
- [Baltic Sea Documentary Forum](https://dokforums.gov.lv/)
- [ArtDocFest](https://artdocfest.com/en/) -- A documentary film festival formerly based in Russia has relocated to Latvia. Their site also hosts a rich digital [cinemateque](https://artdoc.media/en) where many films are available, either for free or with a subscription.
- [European Documentary Film Symposiums](https://www.latfilma.lv/symposium/) -- Active from 1977 until the 2000s, this forum is now attempting a revival at ArtDocFest.
- [Lielais Kristaps] -- National Film Award, previously National Film Festival

## Film Museums

## Film Studios
- [Riga Film Studio](https://www.studio.lv/lv/) --Formerly one of the biggest film studios in the USSR, it had an extraordinary documentary branch. Currently, it deals with preservation and digitalization.
- [VFS films](https://www.vfs.lv/) -- produced "The Jump", "Bridges of Time", "Ukrainian Sheriffs" and many others.
- [Juris Podnieks Studio](https://www.jps.lv/) -- originally founded by Juris Podnieks, is now headed by Antra Cilinska, documentary filmmaker, producer, editor, Juris' long-time collaborator
- [GILDE](https://www.latfilma.lv/gilde/cilveki.html) -- run by Romualdas Pipars and Baiba Urbane
- [Dream Well Studio](https://www.dreamwell.lv/) -- independent animation production company. Founded by Gints Zilbalodis.
- [Rija Films](https://www.rijafilms.com/) -- has a section of its films available on demand
- [Locomotive](https://locomotive.lv/) -- In addition to film production, the studio conducts outstanding restorations of Latvian film classics
- [List of film production companies and studios at latfima](https://web.archive.org/web/20190222012151/http://latfilma.lv/0stu.html)
